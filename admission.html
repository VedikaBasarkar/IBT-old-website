Admisssion

How to Apply

  1. The applicant has to apply for admission only through the prescribed form available with the prospectus.
  2. The prospectus, identity card and information brochure may be purchased from the college offices.
  3. Parent's/father's/Mother's undertaking is compulsory.
  4. Students must write their detailed address and contact number on the admission form.
  5. The following documents (dually attested Photostat copies) must accompany the prescribed application form.
  6. Admission form fees for PG courses Rs.500/- for Open Category (Rs.350/- for Reserved).
  7. Admission form fees for UG courses Rs.300/- for Open Catagory (Rs.200/- For Reserved).
  8. Registration fees for any course Rs.500/-.
Before submitting the original certificates in the college, students should take out photo copies of the documents for there convinces
  1. Mark sheet
  2. Caste Certificate if Applicable.
  3. Leaving / transfer certificate (TC) (Original certificate of institute last attended. Duplicate TC will not be entertained).
  4. Migration certificate : (for the applicant from other university)
  5. Latest 3 passport size photographs
