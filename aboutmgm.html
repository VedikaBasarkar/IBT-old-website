About MGM and IBT

MAHATMA GANDHI MISSION

The Mahatma Gandhi Mission Trust was established on 20th December, 1982 at Nanded for enhancing the level of educational opportunities available to the people of Marathwada region. Since inception, the Trust has done outstanding service to Society by providing education in Medical, Engineering, Architecture, Nursing, Management, Computer Science & IT, Bioinformatics & Biotechnology, Fine arts & Journalism and primary education with upmost dedication and commitment. Phenomenal growth of educational institutions under its wings at Aurangabad, Navi Mumbai and Parbhani in Maharashtra and Noida (New Delhi) in UP bears testimony to the fact that the trust is constantly alive to and is meeting ever increasing aspirations of society in the field of education. Concern for Promotion and preservation of Indian culture, Gandhian values and sports is central to the vision of the Trust. Creation of contemporary academic environment and state of the art institutional infrastructure by the trust reflects their resolve to produce human resource which would contribute positively to the society, nation and the humanity. The Trust takes its strength from its Chairman Honable Shri Kamal Kishore Kadam, former Education Minister of Maharashtra. An alumni of IIT Mumbai, Mr. Kadam is a highly experienced academician and educationist. All founder members of the trust are academicians, professionals and visionaries inspired by Late Shri Nanasaheb Kadam, an ardent follower of the Gandhian ideology headed by Shri. Kamal Kishore Kadam. For the last three decades, Mahatma Gandhi Mission (MGM) has blazed a trail of academic excellence and state- of-the-art health care services. With its brilliant academic and administrative fraternity coupled with its modern infrastructure, Mahatma Gandhi Mission educational institutions are and ideal and exciting choice for education.

Institute of Biosciences and Technology

The Mahatma Gandhi Mission Institute of Biosciences and Technology is promoted by Mahatma Gandhi Mission (MGM) Trust. The Mahatma Gandhi Mission Trust was founded with a vision to improve the Educational, Health and other Social needs of the public in general since 1983. In fact, MGM does not limit itself when it comes to serving for the advancement of public in general. To live by its aims and objectives, the trust has promoted many Educational Institutions and Health Services. Overall MGM Campus consist of 70 Acres landscape and is full of natural beauty, shady trees and is having the comfortable sitting arrangement under these trees which naturally creates and promotes studious environment. MGM visualized the density of the field of life science resources and career which will be helpful in the area of research. Through the keen interest MGM aimed to establish the department of Biotechnology and Bioinformatics. In the year 2001-2002 the department of Biotechnology and Bioinformatics was established with the vision to provide knowledge-based technological services to satisfy the needs of society and the industry with the help of Biotech areas. The department had laid its first milestone with the course of diploma Bioinformatics (PG Diploma in Bioinformatics). With the intake of 20 students the course was completed satisfactorily. Then in the year 2002-2003 with the affiliation under Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Marathwada University, Aurangabad PG course M.Sc. Biotechnology was started which was a biggest milestone of MGM in the area of Biotechnology. The course was started with the intake of 20 students. The college was started with limited resources and laboratory facilities. In the year 2004-05 MGM launched a course B.Sc. Agricultural Biotechnology under the affiliation of Marathwada Krishi Vidyapeeth, Parbhani. With the launch of this course the department of biotechnology and Bioinformatics become crown in the area of Marathwada region. A small root turned into an huge tree with all the branches. In the year 2005-2006 MGM visualized the urge of field of informatics. Keeping the attitude to excel the field of biotechnology, the course of M.Sc. Bioinformatics was launched under the affiliation of Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Marathwada University, Aurangabad. Now MGM is well established in the field of research in the area of biotechnology and Bioinformatics with well equipped R & D laboratory to excel the name of Mahatma Gandhi Mission on the globe. This institute with the impartation of new academics procures the moral and ethical values of golden Indian culture and support India to become a leading nation

Affiliation And Accredition

The Mahatma Gandhi Mission Institute of Biosciences and Technology is affiliated to Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Marathwada univesrity, Aurangabad.
